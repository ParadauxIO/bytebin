Report Abuse

If you have encountered content that violates the law or our terms of service, please let us know.

Contact

To report abusive, illegal, or otherwise objectionable content hosted on bytebin, please contact us via email:

Abuse contact
abuse@paradaux.io

What to include

To help us investigate and act on your report as quickly as possible, please include the following in your email:

  • The full URL(s) of the content in question.
  • A description of the issue (e.g. malware, copyright infringement, illegal content, personal data exposure).
  • Your contact information so we can follow up if needed.
  • If applicable, any relevant legal notices or takedown requests (e.g. DMCA).

Response

We take abuse reports seriously and aim to review all reports promptly. Content that violates our terms of service or applicable law will be removed.

We reserve the right to remove any content at our sole discretion, without prior notice.

Automated abuse: If you are a hosting provider, CERT, or law enforcement agency and need to report abuse at scale, please contact us at the email above and we will work with you to resolve the issue.
